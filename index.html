How it works

Automatic. Silent. Permanent.

Your team changes nothing. WyntIQ runs silently in the background and builds your company's intelligence layer from day one.

01
One time, 30 minutes
Connect your company tools
An admin connects your existing tools in a single session. Your team does nothing.
Connects to: Slack, Notion, Jira, Confluence, GitHub

Email, any provider: Gmail, Outlook, or your own company domain. Works via SMTP/IMAP with Zoho Mail, Google Workspace, GoDaddy, Hostinger, or any private mail server.

Coming soon: Zoom transcripts, Microsoft Teams
02
Automatic, Continuous, Silent
AI reads and extracts decisions
WyntIQ reads your messages, emails, docs and tickets silently. It identifies what was decided, why, who made the call, and the broader context.
Extracts: Decisions made, reasons behind them, who was involved, project context, vendor and client relationships

Never captures: Personal DMs, HR sensitive conversations, anything outside your defined work channels
03
Structured, Linked, Tagged
Knowledge is organised automatically
Every decision is tagged to the right person, project, and date. No folders to maintain. No manual work ever.
Example from one Slack message:
"Going with Razorpay over Stripe — Rahul's call — better UPI support and GST invoicing for India."

Decision: Razorpay. Reason: UPI and GST. Decision maker: Rahul. Source: Slack with link.
04
Plain English, Instant, Sourced
Ask anything. Get a clear answer.
Anyone in leadership types a question in plain English and gets an immediate, sourced answer.
Works instantly for: "Why did we choose this vendor?" "Who owns the payment integration?" "What was decided in Q3?" "Why was this feature delayed?" "Who is our AWS contact?"
05
Automated, When it matters most
Someone resigns — full handoff in 30 seconds
When HR logs a resignation, WyntIQ auto generates a complete structured handoff report covering everything that person knew, owned, and was responsible for.
Handoff includes: All projects and status, key decisions and why, contacts and vendors, open tasks, recommended handover per item

Export as: PDF, Word, Shareable link

Common questions
Do employees need to install or do anything?+
Nothing at all. Only an admin connects tools during the initial 30 minute setup. Employees carry on as normal.
We use our own company domain email. Will it work?+
Yes, completely. WyntIQ connects via SMTP and IMAP which works with Zoho Mail, Google Workspace, GoDaddy, Hostinger, or any private mail server. No Gmail required.
Is this a chatbot or a documentation tool?+
Neither. WyntIQ is a Company Intelligence System that captures why decisions were made, who made them, and what the context was. Fundamentally different from a chatbot or a tool that requires manual input.
What about employee privacy?+
You define exactly which channels are indexed during setup. Personal DMs and HR sensitive conversations are never included. Every employee can view their own knowledge profile at any time.
How long before we see value?+
Setup takes 30 minutes. The AI processes your existing history in 24 to 48 hours. Most customers get their first useful answer within the first day.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Monthly plans cancel anytime with no penalty. Annual plans cancel with 30 days notice.

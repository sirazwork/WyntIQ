The Problem

India's logistics runs
on paper and phone calls.

01 / DEFENCE
Days to Procure a Single Spare Part
A technician raises a demand. It travels by paper — officer, logistics, accounts, vendor — each adding days. Aircraft stay grounded. Missions are delayed. The enemy doesn't wait.
✈️
02 / VISIBILITY
Nobody Knows What's Where
Supplies at Ladakh. Vehicles in Pune. Parts in Chennai. No single system connects them. Commanders make decisions blind. Duplicates pile up at one base while another runs out.
🗺️
03 / CONNECTIVITY
Cloud Systems Die at the Edge
Every existing logistics platform needs internet. When jamming hits, when towers fall, when you're in Siachen — your software stops working exactly when you need it most.
📡
04 / ACCOUNTABILITY
Zero Audit Trail
Who took the part? When? Which aircraft? Approved by whom? Manual systems leave no trail. Accountability disappears. Investigations take weeks. Fraud goes undetected.

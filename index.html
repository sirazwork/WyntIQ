Common questions
Do employees need to install or do anything? +
Nothing at all. Only an admin connects tools during the initial 30-minute setup. Employees carry on as normal — they won't even know it's running.
We use our own company domain email. Will it work? +
Yes, completely. WyntIQ connects via SMTP and IMAP — the standard protocols used by every email provider. Works with Zoho Mail, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, GoDaddy, Hostinger, or any private mail server. You provide the server address and port during setup. No Gmail required.
Is this a chatbot or a documentation tool? +
Neither. WyntIQ is a Company Intelligence System — it captures why decisions were made, who made them, and what the context was. Fundamentally different from a chatbot or a documentation tool that requires manual input.
What about employee privacy? +
You define exactly which channels are indexed during setup. Personal DMs and HR-sensitive conversations are never included. Every employee can view their own knowledge profile at any time.
How long before we see value? +
Setup takes 30 minutes. The AI processes your existing history in 24–48 hours. Most customers get their first useful answer within the first day — usually something their team has been trying to find for weeks.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Monthly plans cancel anytime with no penalty. Annual plans cancel with 30 days notice.

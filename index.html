Core Product

Operational modules for field logistics.

Start with inventory and demands, then connect assets, QR workflows, maps, audit, readiness, and sync diagnostics around the same operational record.

INV

Inventory and Demands

Raise, approve, reject, and issue with real accountability, role control, and smart stock bypass where local inventory already exists.

AST

Asset Tracking

Track vehicles, kits, crates, and critical equipment by status, location, custodian, and last movement without relying on cloud access.

AUD

Tamper-Aware Audit

Every meaningful action is linked to identity, role, and time so commanders and operators can trust the record under pressure.

MAP

Map and Theater View

Show operating picture, relay nodes, delivery routes, and asset positions when connectivity exists, while preserving local truth when it does not.

QR

QR Workflow

Generate scannable tags for demands and assets so field teams can verify the right item, package, or unit without paperwork friction.

OPS

Readiness Scoring

Turn stock health, asset serviceability, demand backlog, and network path status into one picture leaders can act on quickly.

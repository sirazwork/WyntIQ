Cost Drivers

What affects the final quote?

WYNTIQ is not priced like a simple website subscription because deployments can differ sharply across defence units, remote industrial sites, and emergency operations.

Users and roles

Operators, officers, technicians, logistics users, auditors, and administrators may need different access structures.

Sites and devices

Single-site trials are simpler than multi-site rollouts with distributed devices and field teams.

Offline requirements

Disconnected operation, local-first deployment, and sync paths influence setup and support scope.

Support level

Training, onboarding, custom configuration, and deployment assistance can be included based on need.

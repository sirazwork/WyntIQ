Cost Drivers

What affects the final product price?

WYNTIQ quotes stay simple: the commercial scope is driven by named users, with devices and sites included in the license scope.

Users

Operators, officers, technicians, logistics users, auditors, and administrators included in the license scope.

Devices

Unlimited approved devices are included, so teams can work across desktops, laptops, tablets, and field terminals.

Sites

Operational sites are included, making the quote easier for multi-location teams to understand.

Activation

One license can support online verification and offline activation for disconnected working conditions.

Cost Drivers

What affects the final product price?

WYNTIQ quotes stay simple: the commercial scope is driven by users, devices, and sites, with activation prepared for the required working conditions.

Users

Operators, officers, technicians, logistics users, auditors, and administrators included in the license scope.

Devices

Approved machines or terminals that need access to the WYNTIQ product.

Sites

Bases, plants, mines, depots, units, or operating locations covered by the deployment.

Activation

One license can support online verification and offline activation for disconnected working conditions.

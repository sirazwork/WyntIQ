Why TwinStance Solutions?

Most software jobs ask you to build a feature for a dashboard that someone may or may not notice. At TwinStance Solutions, the software you build is used by IAF technicians maintaining aircraft that protect India's airspace, Army logistics officers managing supplies to soldiers in Ladakh, and maintenance engineers at mine sites where a breakdown costs ₹10 lakhs per hour. Your work has direct operational impact.

We are a small, focused team building a genuinely hard product — offline-first synchronisation, cryptographic audit trails, AI-powered bottleneck detection, role-based access control that actually matches how defence organisations work. These are real engineering challenges, not CRUD applications dressed up with a React frontend.

TwinStance Solutions is headquartered in Cuttack, Odisha — and we are proud of it. We are building the future of Indian defence technology from Odisha, not from a tech park in Bengaluru. If you are from Odisha, or if you believe that great technology companies can be built outside the traditional tech capitals, you will feel at home here.

Our Values
What We Stand For

TwinStance Solutions is built on a set of values that are not slogans — they are the actual principles we use to make decisions about the product, the team, and the company.

🎯
Operational Reality First
We build for how organisations actually work, not how we wish they worked. Our product decisions are grounded in real operational experience from defence and enterprise environments — not in what looks good in a demo.
🔒
Accountability Without Exception
Every action in WYNTIQ creates a permanent, tamper-proof record. We hold ourselves to the same standard internally — every decision is made with full ownership, and every commitment is meant and kept.
🇮🇳
India First, Always
We are building an Indian product for India's most critical sectors. Data sovereignty, Atmanirbhar Bharat, and iDEX alignment are not marketing points — they are fundamental to why TwinStance Solutions exists.
📡
Offline Before Online
Our commitment to offline-first design is not just a technical choice — it is a statement about who we build for. The soldier in Ladakh and the mine worker in Jharkhand deserve the same quality of tool as the executive in Delhi.
🧠
Explain, Don't Just Report
The difference between a tracking tool and an intelligent system is whether it tells you what happened or why it happened. We build tools that explain decisions and help people improve — not just tools that record history.
🤝
Small Team, Big Ownership
Every person on the TwinStance Solutions team has broad responsibility and genuine ownership. There are no people who just execute tickets. Everyone thinks about the product, everyone understands the customer, everyone contributes to the mission.

Backend Engineer — Sync & Data
Full TimeRemote / Cuttack
Engineering · Infrastructure
WYNTIQ's offline-first synchronisation engine is the most technically complex part of the platform. When hundreds of devices operate independently for hours or days and then sync back to a central server, the data reconciliation problem is genuinely hard — handling conflicts, preserving audit integrity, resolving eventual consistency issues, and doing all of this reliably on low-bandwidth connections. You will own this core infrastructure, build it to production quality, and ensure it is robust enough for defence and industrial deployments where data loss is never acceptable. Experience with distributed systems, CRDT data structures, event sourcing, or conflict-free replicated data types is a strong advantage.
